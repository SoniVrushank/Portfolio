campaign_check.sql
-- weekly performance check SELECT campaign_name, ROUND(100.0 * clicks / impressions, 2) AS ctr, ROUND(100.0 * conversions / clicks, 2) AS cvr FROM ad_campaigns WHERE platform IN ('meta', 'google') ORDER BY cvr DESC;

In plain English: this is the kind of check that shows whether Meta or Google is actually converting better this week — before the budget gets spent finding out the hard way.

05 — How & why I work

Most marketers guess and call it a strategy. I check the numbers first, then write the ad, the post, or the page. A Power BI report is as familiar to me as a content calendar — that combination is rarer than it should be.

01

Research

Look at the data before touching the creative — who's converting, who isn't, and why.

02

Strategy

Turn that into an actual plan — channels, budget split, what success looks like.

03

Execute

Write it, design it, launch it — Meta, Google, content, whatever the plan calls for.

04

Measure

Back into Power BI or the dashboard — check what worked, adjust the next cycle.

07 — Founder, Heave Group

Heave Group started from something personal — growing up around a jewellery business meant seeing, up close, how much of retail still runs on guesswork and paper. JewifyLabs is the direct answer to that: a jewellery-showroom ERP built from the inside. HeaveOs (a personal AI assistant), HeavifyLabs (a digital marketing agency), GrowthifyLabs (AI-powered business analysis software), and CommunifyLabs (community building through WhatsApp and Meta Business Suite) grew out from that same instinct — five ventures, one idea: build the tool you wished existed when you needed it.
